The Webb Schools

Founded in 1912 by Thompson Webb, The Webb Schools from Claremont, California strives to provide students with the best academics, team sports, outdoor adventure, and participation in the arts, as well as training students with our unique "Unbounded Thinking."

Admissions

Webb celebrate diversity of thought and encourage our students to be themselves. Webb students want to be in an environment where they’re challenged to push boundaries; they know they have freedom to take intellectual risks to learn, grow and develop into moral leaders.

Alf Museum

Webb is the only high school in the United States to have a Paleontology Museum on its campus. The Alf Museum is also a perfect example of unbounded thinking in action. After all, 95% of the museum’s more than 175,000 specimens were discovered by Webb students, teachers and alumni.
