An Innovative Curriculum

Webb offers a rigorous academic program in Science, Mathematics, Humanities, World languages, and Fine arts. Students will choose from a full range of courses, including honors, Advanced Placement and Advanced Studies, while being encouraged to develop and pursue their passions and interests.

Webb is a Team Sport

Webb has a competitive interscholastic athletics program for students of all skill levels with 42 teams participating in 15 sports. As one of the most competitive schools in its league and division, Webb traditionally qualifies teams and individuals to post season competition, owning 131 league titles and 3 CIF titles.
